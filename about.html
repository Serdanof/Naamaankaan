Know Who We Are

Naamaankaan is a Pvt Ltd firm, that has been working in the education industry for years, for the development of students to have their dream college in the entire world. It started with one of the home students to the broad of various students across the globe.
Naamaankaan is one of the leading education consultant teams in India with its head office in Patna. We have developed thoroughly and have impressively coped with the changing educational environment. With our help and guidance, numerous aspirants were able to get admission to top universities throughout the world. We have a team of highly dedicated and goal-oriented individuals who work strenuously for students’ betterment.
Other than Patna, we have our operational center in various cities in India. Moreover, with the help of our partners, we are planning to increase our number of branches throughout the country. Our executives represent top-rated universities in various countries like the USA, Australia, the UK, Germany, Russia, China, Poland, etc. All these universities are highly regarded because of the top-notch education they impart. Our executives ensure that the students are under supervision not only in India but abroad too. Proper assistance and guidance are provided at every step to all the students.
Apart from helping the students to reach their educational destination, our team members have expertise in bestowing high-quality counseling services to the students.
Unlike others, we don’t end our relationship with the students after their admission. We provide what we say, " A step ahead". Even after the student’s admission, our team members are available to resolve any problem they encounter.
